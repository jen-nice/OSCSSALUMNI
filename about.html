About

Went to the Ontario Science Centre Science School?


Welcome Alumni! We have created this website to foster a community of OSCSS alumni so we can meet each other, share our experiences and maybe even go ahead and create new ones! Welcome! Don't forget to register so we know you've been here!



What the OSCSS is about?


The OSCSS is a program where you get to spend a semester of your 12th grade studying at the one and only Ontario Science Centre, while earning school science and math credits! How cool is that? For more information and how to apply, go to this website .We promise, it's worth every second!
